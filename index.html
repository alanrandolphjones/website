About me

Hello! My name is Alan.

I am a front-end developer in Toronto, Canada with a background in writing and film. I love the Internet, making web applications, watching movies and listening to Drake. I'm a natural-born problem-solver, which comes in handy when I'm building websites and creating memorable user experiences.

Prior to web development, I worked as a writer and editor. You can find my journalism in The Globe and Mail, VICE, Maisonneuve Magazine and many other publications. I also have two (!) degrees in film studies and worked as an editor for Screen Rant. In my off-hours, you can probably find me online sharing the latest Nicolas Cage memes.
